Privacy Policy — River Runner

Effective date: 26 May 2026

River Runner ("the app") is an offline arcade game published by Cloud Kick Games. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — handle. Read it together with the in-app behaviour: nothing in this document overrides what the app actually does on your device.

1. Data we collect

None. River Runner does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an account or sign-in.
  • Does not include analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any other third-party data collectors.
  • Does not make any network requests at runtime.
  • Does not use cookies (it is not a web app).

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device, using Android's standard SharedPreferences mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. It includes:

  • Your high score.
  • Your sound-effect on/off preference.
  • Your music on/off preference.

You can remove this data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device's Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

3. Permissions the app uses

The app uses the device's motion sensors (accelerometer) for tilt-based controls. Sensor readings are processed on-device in real time and are not stored or transmitted.

4. Children's privacy

River Runner is suitable for general audiences. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it also collects none from children.

5. Third-party services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or external APIs at runtime.

6. Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes its data behaviour, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes on Google Play.

7. Contact
