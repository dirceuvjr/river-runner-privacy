Privacy Policy — River Runner

Effective date: 15 June 2026

River Runner ("the app") is an arcade game published by Cloud Kick Games. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — handle. Read it together with the in-app behaviour: nothing in this document overrides what the app actually does on your device.

The app is fully playable offline. It includes optional online features — leaderboards and achievements — powered by Google Play Games Services. These only ever activate if you choose to sign in. If you never sign in, the app collects and transmits no data at all.

1. Data we collect

If you do not sign in to Google Play Games: none. Without sign-in, River Runner does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, makes no network requests for gameplay, and includes no analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, or crash reporters.

If you choose to sign in to Google Play Games (an optional action you start from the menu or settings), the app uses Google Play Games Services to provide leaderboards and achievements. To do this, the following is processed by Google Play Games Services:

  • Your Google Play Games identifier (player ID) and gamertag.
  • Your game progress: your scores (submitted to the leaderboard) and your achievement progress.

Cloud Kick Games does not operate its own servers and does not separately receive, store, or sell this data. It is handled by Google Play Games Services on your behalf to run the leaderboard and achievement features. See section 4.

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device, using Android's standard SharedPreferences mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. It includes:

  • Your high score.
  • Your control-scheme choice (tilt / buttons / swipe).
  • Your sound-effect on/off preference.
  • Your music on/off preference.

You can remove this data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device's Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

3. Permissions the app uses

  • Motion sensors (accelerometer) — for tilt-based controls. Sensor readings are processed on-device in real time and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Network (internet) access — used solely for Google Play Games Services when you are signed in (leaderboards and achievements). It is not used for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

4. Google Play Games Services

The app's optional sign-in, leaderboards, and achievements are provided by Google Play Games Services, a service operated by Google. When you sign in, your interaction with leaderboards and achievements is governed by Google's own privacy policy and terms:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Signing in is optional and can be declined; the game remains fully playable without it. You can manage or sign out of your Google Play Games profile, and control what it shares, through the Google Play Games app and your Google Account settings. Removing your leaderboard scores or achievement progress is done through Google Play Games, as that data is held by Google's service rather than by us.

5. Children's privacy

River Runner is suitable for general audiences. With no sign-in, the app collects no personal information from anyone, including children. Signing in to Google Play Games is optional and is subject to Google's account eligibility and age requirements; that sign-in and any data it involves are handled by Google.

6. Third-party services

The only third-party service the app integrates is Google Play Games Services, and only when you choose to sign in (see section 4). The app integrates no advertising, analytics, or other third-party SDKs.

7. Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes its data behaviour, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes on Google Play.
