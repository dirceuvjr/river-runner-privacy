Data Deletion — River Runner

River Runner, published by Cloud Kick Games · Last updated: 15 June 2026

This page explains how to delete the data associated with River Runner and what is removed or kept. Cloud Kick Games operates no servers of its own: River Runner stores some data on your device, and — only if you choose to sign in to Google Play Games — your scores and achievements are held by Google Play Games. There is no separate copy on our side.

1. Delete the data stored on your device

This removes your local high score and your in-app settings immediately:

  • Open your device Settings → Apps → River Runner → Storage and tap Clear storage (sometimes "Clear data"); or
  • Uninstall River Runner.

We never receive this on-device data, so once you clear it or uninstall, it is gone with no retention on our side.

2. Delete your leaderboard & achievement data

This applies only if you signed in to Google Play Games. Your Google Play Games identifier, submitted scores, and achievement progress are stored by Google Play Games, not by Cloud Kick Games. You can delete this through Google:

  1. Open the Google Play Games app on your device.
  2. Go to your profile, then Settings.
  3. Choose the option to delete Play Games data — you can delete the data for River Runner specifically, or your entire Google Play Games profile and its data. You can also manage and delete this data from your Google Account.

For your security, data held under your Google Account can be removed only by you, through Google. Deleting it removes your scores and achievement progress for River Runner from Google Play Games.

3. Ask us for help

If you would like help or want to confirm what is stored, email dev.dirceuvjr+riverrunner@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion request." Because we keep no data on our own servers, we will confirm what is held and guide you through the steps above.

What is deleted and what is kept

  • On-device data (high score, control scheme, sound settings): deleted immediately when you clear storage or uninstall. We never receive it; no retention on our side.
  • Google Play Games data (player identifier, scores, achievements): held by Google until you delete it via Google Play Games or your Google Account. We retain no separate copy and apply no additional retention period.
